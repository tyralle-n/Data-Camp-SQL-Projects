# Student Mental Health Analysis

This project explores mental health survey data from university students to identify patterns in stress, academic pressure, and help‑seeking behavior.

## Key Questions
- How do stress levels vary across academic years?
- Are there differences between genders?
- What factors influence whether students seek help?

## Skills Demonstrated
- Filtering and grouping (WHERE, GROUP BY)
- Calculating averages and distributions (AVG, COUNT)
- Comparing demographic groups
- Extracting insights from survey‑based data
